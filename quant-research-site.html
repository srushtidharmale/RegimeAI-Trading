My 5-Month Research Journey

Month 1: Foundation

Problem Framing & Data Inventory

Established research hypotheses, identified data sources, and created systematic approach to testing Carver's methodologies. Built comprehensive dataset covering major futures markets.

Month 2: Signal Research

Signal Development & Falsification

Developed and tested multiple signal types including momentum, carry, and mean reversion strategies. Applied rigorous statistical testing to avoid overfitting.

Month 3: Portfolio Construction

Robust Portfolio Design

Implemented correlation-aware position sizing, risk budgeting, and diversification techniques. Created multi-strategy portfolio with dynamic allocation.

Month 4: System Integrity

Implementation & Risk Controls

Built comprehensive risk management framework, transaction cost modeling, and system monitoring. Developed kill-switches and drawdown protection mechanisms.

Month 5: Live-Sim Readiness

Governance & Board Presentation

Prepared live simulation environment, created governance documentation, and developed board-ready materials with clear decision points and risk frameworks.
