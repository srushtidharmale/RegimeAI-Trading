The Carver Legacy

Robert Carver's Revolutionary Approach

Robert Carver transformed quantitative finance by emphasizing robustness over complexity. His "Advanced Futures Trading Strategies" became the gold standard for systematic trading, teaching us that simple, well-tested strategies often outperform complex models.

2015
"Systematic Trading"
Carver's first major work establishing the foundation for systematic trading. Introduced concepts of position sizing, risk management, and the importance of robust testing methodologies.
2017
"Advanced Futures Trading Strategies"
The masterpiece that revolutionized futures trading. Comprehensive coverage of trend following, carry strategies, and mean reversion with practical implementation guidance and real-world examples.
2019
"Leveraged Trading"
Advanced concepts in leveraged trading, focusing on optimal leverage, risk budgeting, and the mathematics of compound returns in leveraged strategies.
2021
"Smart Portfolios"
Modern portfolio construction techniques, incorporating machine learning while maintaining the core principles of robustness and simplicity.
